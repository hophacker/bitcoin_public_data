Description


F.A.M.E. (abbreviation of Fans Are My Everything) is the upcoming fourth studio album by American recording artist Chris Brown, due for a release on March 18, 2011 through Jive Records. Brown worked with a variety of producers and writers such as, Benny Benassi, DJ Frank E, Kevin McCall and Diplo, among others.
"Yeah 3x" was released as the album's first single on October 25, 2010. It served as the pop single for mainstream audiences, and went on to top the singles chart of New Zealand. It also reached the top ten in the United Kingdom, Ireland and Australia. The second single "Look at Me Now" was released as the hip-hop single for urban audiences on February 1, 2011. Featuring guest vocals from American rappers Lil Wayne and Busta Rhymes, the single peaked within the top fifteen in the United States. In support of the album, Brown will embark on his F.A.M.E Tour beginning in Australia in April 2011.
