Happy Easter to all of my Christian Brothers and Sisters out there reading this today. It is my prayer that you allow Him to set deep roots in your heart, and that perhaps by some small measure, He uses the messages within these Christian songs to keep you anchored, obedient, and humble.

For anyone interested, here's a quick break down of this bundle. Again, I may be off on some of my genres, but I have these listed accordingly. How about Anthem Lights and Michael English? Under Pop you might enjoy Aprilemade, David Archuleta, and Shawn McDonald. Under Praise & Worship you might enjoy Bridge Music, and The Orchard Church. How about DJ Cmd. Alternative? How about LifePoint Music. Or maybe you just want to relax to a nice Instrumental, then check out The Reveille.
