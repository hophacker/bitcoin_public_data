There's still some confusion.... so to be clear!!! BBC 2012 - COMPLETE Series MEANS....all of the episodes from the year 2012. The term SERIES in North America means all the episodes EVER broadcast. For example DEXTER was on for 8 years. The entire SERIES would be all episodes from all 8 years. HOWEVER....in Europe (Where the BBC is !!! ) the term Series means all the episodes for that year (also known as a SEASON in North America). Using the same example Dexter had 8 Series running from 2006 to 2013. This file is the complete (EUROPEAN Definition) 2012 Series. Or put another way...the Complete 2012 SEASON.
