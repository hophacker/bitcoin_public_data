Description

Greatest Hits is claimed to be a rare 500 copy greatest hits album by Canadian rock band Nickelback.It is the
bands second compilation album (the first being Three-Sided Coin). It contains tracks from all six of the bands'
albums. Given the availability of the release through only a single obscure retailer, and the artwork that shows a
distributor other than the band's official label, it is probable if not certain that this is a pirate album, not an
official release.
