J2XML 2.5 RC 1 will be released soon

Details
Created: 25 February 2013

The wait is almost over: after 10 months of work, the first public release of J2XML 2.5 will finally be published on

March 1st.

J2XML 2.5 is a full version of J2XML for Joomla 2.5. It allows you to export/import data from/to Joomla 2.5. And using the J2XML Importer 1.5 plugin you can import data from j2xml 1.5.3.
