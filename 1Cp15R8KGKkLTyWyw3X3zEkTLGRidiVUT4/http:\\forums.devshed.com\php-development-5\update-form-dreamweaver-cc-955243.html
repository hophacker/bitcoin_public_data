    Is there anything you're interested in? A specific type of PHP application you'd like to try building? Most people start with something like a login page, a form mailer, or something similar. Something to begin building. But start small. Don't start learning with the intention of building a blog or MVC framework. Build your house one wall at a time. Basically, start off with something that you can tinker with. Make it work. Post back here and get suggestions on ways to improve it. This way you'll learn by doing, not by watching tutorials alone. I agree with Jacques that most tutorials are terribly written by amateurs who really don't know what they are doing. And the ones who do, generally don't have enough space or time to explain something thoroughly.

    You should also dedicate a bit of study on coding standards and general good practices. This can help you get off on the right foot. The rest you'll learn as you go (and mostly by trial and error!)
