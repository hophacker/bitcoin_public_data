OUT-OF-PRINT LIBRARY



The Ite ad Thomam Out-of-Print Library (ITOPL) is a massive collection of out-of-print, "out-dated," hard-to-find books on a variety of subjects related to traditional Catholic philosophy and theology.  We have more than 100 GB worth of PDF files.  The collection is ideal for your tablet or notebook PC, or as a reference or research library for your laptop or desktop.  Scroll down to see our catalogue of PDFs.


To Obtain the Entire Collection

If you want the whole collection, it will be delivered in the mail in a portable hard drive (between 300 GB and 500 GB capacity, to fit our 110 GB collection), which you get to keep. Please include your mailing address in the order.

Requested donation for the entire collection: $599 USD.

(Includes external hard drive plus + international shipping/handling).

Because we want to make this collection available to everyone, we offer interest-free payment plans and ship almost anywhere in the world for free.  To obtain the entire collection, please select a payment plan and click the Paypal Buy Now button and follow the prompts:


Payment Options

All payments are made through PayPal.  If you wish to pay using any major debit or credit card and do not have a PayPal account, you can create a free PayPal account very quickly and easily: just click the Buy Now button above, look for the "Create a Paypal Account" link, and follow the prompts.  Or, if you want to use a different form of payment other than Paypal (such as check or money order), if you wish to make a personalized payment arrangement, or if you have any questions, please send us an email to the address below.  In your email, please do not include philosophical/theological inquiries; if you have such an inquiry, please send it in a separate email, for quicker response time.


Your non-tax-deductible gift to Ite ad Thomam covers for the expenses of the project (viz., shipping/handling; cost of external hard drives; and the many, long hours that are put into the project of acquiring the volumes, scanning them, packaging them, and mailing them). By giving to Ite ad Thomam, you would be helping us further the cause of Scholastic Thomism.



To Obtain Individual Files

To obtain individual files, use the Paypal button below.  When prompted, just copy from the list below the names of the files in which you are interested, and include them in the form.  Be sure also to indicate the quantity of files that you wish to order in the "quantity" box. Note: This is not an automated service. I just get an email notifying me of the payment, and I then have to upload the file manually. Plus I have a very consuming job as a professor and chair of a humanities department, and I do not check my iteadthomam email account every day, so it may take a day or so for me to find out and send you the file. Patience is appreciated. But look at the bright side: you get personalized attention from a real person, and not from an automated service. ;o)

Requested donation per file. $9 USD.  

If you wish to use a different form of payment other than Paypal (such as check, or money-order), or have any other questions, please send us an email to the address below.


Individual files (up to 50) will be delivered electronically.



Copyright Issues

I am offering these texts assuming that what I'm doing is legal. As far as I understand, the items in the list are now public domain because their copyright has expired. But I am not a legal expert, and if you are aware of any copyright laws protecting any of these texts, please let us know and I will remove that text from the list. (I have already been made aware of a couple of texts that are still protected, even if they are out-of-print. I kindly appreciate those who made me aware.)


Amazing Collection

The scans are, for the most part, very good; and even those that are not "excellent" are still very readable. Free sample pages representative of your order are available upon request. Please email Ite ad Thomam for details.
