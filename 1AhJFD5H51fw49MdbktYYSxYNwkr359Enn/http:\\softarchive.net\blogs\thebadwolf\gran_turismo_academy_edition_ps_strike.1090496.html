This is a special edition of Polyphony Digital simulation of automobile, produced to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the collaboration between Sony and Nissan GT Academy in the project.

In the Blu-ray of Gran Turismo 5: Academy Edition are the original game several extras, namely:
Complete Pack (Car Pack, Course Pack, Special Paint Pack, Racing Gear Pack previously left)
Car Pack 2
Car Pack 3 and Course Pack 2
The Nissan GTR led this year by series creator, Kazunori Yamauchi, during the 24 hours of Nurburgring

Gran Turismo chief Kazunori Yamauchi said: "GT Academy has captured the imagination of many Gran Turismo fans. It is exciting to watch what Lucas Ordóñez, Jordan Tresson, Jann Mardenborough and Bryan Heitkotter in the USA, are achieving in their new racing careers with Nissan.

"People can now see that it is possible to make the switch from being a fast gamer to become a real racing driver. We wanted to mark the tremendous success of GT Academy with the very latest version of GT5."
