The New Feature; "FRIENDS" added !

SA F.A.Q. Blog
Now SA offers after The NEW RATING SYSTEM, another feature "FRIENDS" of many coming so soon !



- You can open profile of any member in SA and click "Add to friends" (If you need that.)



- After that he will be informed about your request and he may accept or deny your request.





- Friend list is available in all profiles.



- If you want to remove any of your friends, It's easy to eliminate him/her from your own friend list; Open his/her profile and click "Remove from friends"



- We work now to merge this Feature with The New Rating System Feature (then you can get +0.05 to your Rating if your request of being a Friend to anyone accepted)

Again Thank you all !

Yours SA TEAM
