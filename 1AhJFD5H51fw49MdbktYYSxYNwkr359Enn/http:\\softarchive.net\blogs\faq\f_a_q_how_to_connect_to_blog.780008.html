Q- How to connect to PRIVATE blog ?

A-
For example (with blog http://softarchive.net/blogs/raimovm/):


1) Click to Blog name (in example "Nice blog")




2) Click [ connect ]



3) Done!
Just reload page and all articles from this blog will be available for you.

Q- What if after I clicked "[connect]" and not be able to navigate this blog?

A- It means as soon as Admin is online he/she will give you a permission and you surf his own blog you can PM him as well to inquire.
