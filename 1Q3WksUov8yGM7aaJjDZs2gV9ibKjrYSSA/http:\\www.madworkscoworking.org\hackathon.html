  Information

Whether you're you a diehard Bitcoin fan who's been mining for years or someone who just heard about Dogecoin and thought it was hilarious, you should come out to Madworks on Feb. 15 for a Saturday filled with learning about and hacking on cryptocurrencies. Entry to the 12-hour hackathon is $5 (0.008 BTC/.33 LTC/3200 DOGE) and includes 3 meals, some Dogecoin to get you started, and a chance to win a Bitcoin - currently valued around $850!

1st Prize: 1 BTC

2nd Prize: 0.6 BTC

3rd Prize: 0.3 BTC

Not a hacker? No problem! We're planning an "unconference" in parallel with talks, hands-on demos, discussions, giveaways and more!
